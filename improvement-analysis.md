## E. System Validity Note

**Hypothesis being tested:** Students using these tutoring materials for N days will hit the Day-N score and mastery targets defined in Section C.

**Interpretation rule:** If ≥ 2 of 3 milestones result in PARTIAL or FAIL, the failure is systemic — the content, scheduling, or drill difficulty requires revision, not just increased student effort. In that case, review `diagnostic-checklist.md` error patterns and `learner-feedback-form.md` friction reports before adjusting the curriculum.
