# 60-Day Study Plan — Full Mastery Track

**Goal:** Three full passes, all 3 mock exams, exam simulation in Week 8, all Tier-1★ ≥80%.  
**Daily commitment:** 2–3 hours/day  
**Target exam score:** ≥80%

---

## Exam Week Protocol (Days 57–60)

These four days are designed for confidence maintenance, not new learning.

### Day 57 — Light Review
- Skim `02_textbook/` chapters for Tier-1 topics (15 min each)
- Review your own hand-written programs from Phase 3
- No drills, no timed exercises
- **Time: 1.5h max**

### Day 58 — Syntax Flash Review
- Write key syntax for all 11 topics on one sheet of paper, from memory
- Check accuracy — don't study, just verify what you know
- Update `success-metrics.md` with final scores
- **Time: 1h max**

### Day 59 — Rest + Mental Prep
- No studying
- Prepare materials: pencils, ID, exam location confirmed
- Light physical activity recommended
- Review `acceptance-test.md` checklist — confirm all boxes checked
- **Time: 30 min review only**

### Day 60 — Exam Day
- Arrive 15 minutes early
- Tier-1 first: Loops → Arrays → Functions → Pointers
- Read each question twice before writing
- Show all work; partial credit exists
