## What to Do After Certification

- **Final review (1–2 days before exam):** Do one timed run of Drill L4 (Loops) and Drill L7 (Pointers) — the two highest-stakes topics. No new material.
- **Night before:** Stop studying by 9 pm. Sleep at least 7 hours. Fatigue degrades code-tracing accuracy more than any topic gap will.
- **Exam day:** Bring: student ID, pencil + eraser, C syntax reference sheet (if permitted), analog watch for time management. Arrive 15 minutes early.
